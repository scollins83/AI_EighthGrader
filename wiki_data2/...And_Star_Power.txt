...And Star Power is the third officially released album by Californian experimental rock duo Foxygen. It was released October 14, 2014. It is a double album that follows a loose concept around the fictional band Star Power. Unlike their previous full-length, the album was recorded almost entirely at home and in various LA locations such as the Chateau Marmont and Beverly Hills Hotel.
