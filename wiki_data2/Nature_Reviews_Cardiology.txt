Nature Reviews Cardiology is a peer-reviewed journal for cardiologists. Before April 2009, the journal was titled Nature Clinical Practice Cardiovascular Medicine.
Coverage includes:
acute coronary syndromes
arrhythmias
angina/coronary artery disease
cardiomyopathy/heart failure
concomitant disease
congenital conditions
hypertension
imaging
infection
interventional cardiology
pathology
stroke
surgery/transplantation
thrombosis
valvular disease
vascular disease
general therapies
disease markers
genetics and public health.
