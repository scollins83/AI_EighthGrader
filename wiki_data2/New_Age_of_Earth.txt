New Age of Earth is an album by Ashra. It was released in 1976 on Isadora Records. Although released under the band's moniker, it is a solo album by Ashra member Manuel Gttsching. New Age of Earth is on the list of the Top 25 Most Influential Ambient Albums of All Time.
