Kolontr (Hungarian pronunciation: [kolonta]) is a village in Veszprm county, Hungary.


== Soil pollution disaster ==

On 4 October 2010 a 1.5 - 4 meters-high wave of red mud flooded the village from a plant nearby causing nine deaths and several severe chemical burn injuries. Six bodies were found in the mud and other three victims died later in a hospital after the incident. Hundreds of village inhabitants had to evacuate.
