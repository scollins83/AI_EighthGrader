Signalway Antibody is a biotechnology company based in College Park, Maryland, USA and Nanjing, China. It manufactures and develops antibodies, proteins and kits for laboratory research. It was founded in 2004 by Allen Peng.


== Products ==
Signalway Antibody LLC specializes in newly developed antibodies, including Bub3 Y207 ab, PKM2(phospho-Ser37) ab and b-catenin(phospho-Tyr333) antibody. These antibodies have been shown to play an important role in cancer development. It also covers products in the areas of apoptosis, cell cycle regulation, chromatin, the cytoskeleton, immune system regulation, stem cell research, epigenetics, cancer, neuroscience research, and modification-specific antibodies, as well as the Wnt/Notch/Hedgehog and AKT, Jak/Stat, MAPK, and TGFb/smads pathways.
