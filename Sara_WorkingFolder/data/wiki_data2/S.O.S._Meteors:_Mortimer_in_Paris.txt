S.O.S. Meteors: Mortimer in Paris is the eighth comic book in the Blake and Mortimer series by Edgar P. Jacobs. It was first published in Tintin magazine.


== Synopsis ==
The worlds weather has gone mad, Professor Mortimer responds to an invitation by his friend Professor Labrousse, while travelling to see his friend, Mortimer's cab crashes and he finds himself a new adventure right on his friends doorstep.
