The 1927 Kita Tango earthquake occurred in Kyoto Prefecture on 7 March with a moment magnitude of 7.0. Up to 2,956 people were killed 7,806 were injured. Almost all the houses in Mineyama (now part of Kytango) were destroyed as a result. The earthquake was felt as far away as Tokyo and Kagoshima.
