scytonema is a genus of photosynthetic cyanobacteria that contains over 100 species it grows in filaments that form dark mats many species are aquatic and are either free-floating or grow attached to a submerged substrate while others species grow on terrestrial rocks wood soil or plants scytonema is a nitrogen fixer and can provide fixed nitrogen to the leaves of plants on which it is growing on some species of scytonema form a symbiotic relationship with fungi to produce a lichen
