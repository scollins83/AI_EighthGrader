bruce baden collette (born 1934) is an american ichthyologist
== publications ==
the diversity of fishes : biology evolution and ecology (with gehe s helfman and douglas e facey); malden (mass.) : blackwell science cop 2009
results of the tektite program: ecology of coral reef fishes (with sylvia alice earle); natural history museum los angeles county 1972
== trivia ==
the halfbeak hyporhamphus collettei was named after him
